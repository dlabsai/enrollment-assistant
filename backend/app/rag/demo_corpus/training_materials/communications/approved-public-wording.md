# Approved Public Wording

Use these short scripts when a prospective student needs a clear answer and no source document adds useful detail.

## Job placement

"Demo University supports career preparation, but the university does not guarantee job placement. Career Services can help students prepare materials, explore resources, and practice professional communication."

## Academic policies

"For official academic policies and procedures, please use the Demo University Catalog at https://catalog.demo-university.example.edu/."

## Licensure

"Some programs are designed to support educational preparation for licensure. Requirements vary by state, so students should review the published disclosure for their intended state and discuss their goals with Admissions."
