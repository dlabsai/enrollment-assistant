# Lead Note Quality

Staff notes should help the next advisor understand what happened without exposing unnecessary personal detail.

## Include

- Program interest and degree level
- Preferred learning format
- Prior college or military records mentioned
- Target start date
- Questions asked and resources provided
- Promised follow-up and owner

## Avoid

- Copying sensitive personal details that are not needed for advising
- Making unofficial aid, admission, or transfer-credit promises
- Recording guesses as facts
- Using internal shorthand that another team would not understand
