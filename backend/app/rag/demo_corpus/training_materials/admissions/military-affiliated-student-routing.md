# Military-Affiliated Student Routing

Military-affiliated students may have education benefits, military training records, prior college credit, or sponsor billing questions. Keep the conversation organized by separating academic fit from benefit processing.

## Ask first

- Are you active duty, veteran, spouse, dependent, National Guard, Reserve, or using employer/sponsor benefits?
- Do you have a Joint Services Transcript or other military education record?
- Which program and start date are you considering?

## Route carefully

Admissions can discuss program fit and transcript review. Benefit eligibility, certification, payment timing, and sponsor billing should be routed to the benefits or student accounts team that owns the process.
