# Transfer Credit Evaluation

Transfer-credit conversations should be specific without promising a final result before official review.

## Staff talking points

- Official transcripts are required for final transfer-credit evaluation.
- Unofficial transcripts or grade reports can support an early conversation.
- Credit applicability depends on the student's selected program, course level, grade, credits, and fit with current requirements.
- Students with international records should be routed to the records team for credential-evaluation instructions.
- Do not promise a completion date unless the records team has provided one for that student.

## Recommended wording

"We can review your unofficial transcript for early planning, but official transcripts are required before transfer credit is finalized. The records team determines how prior coursework applies to your selected program."
