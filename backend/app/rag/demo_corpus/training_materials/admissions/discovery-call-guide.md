# Discovery Call Guide

Use this guide when a prospective student is still exploring. The goal is not to force a program match in the first answer; it is to collect enough context to compare realistic options.

## Ask one question at a time

Start with the student's academic goal. If the goal is unclear, ask what subject or work setting interests them. After that, ask about degree level, prior college credit, preferred format, and target start date.

## Good discovery sequence

1. What subject or field are you hoping to study?
2. Are you looking for an undergraduate, graduate, or certificate option?
3. Have you completed college credit that you want reviewed?
4. Do you prefer online, campus, or hybrid learning?
5. When would you like to begin?

## Avoid

- Promising admission before file review
- Guessing transfer-credit outcomes
- Quoting exact tuition or aid amounts from memory
- Treating a career goal as a guaranteed employment outcome
