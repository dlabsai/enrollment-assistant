# Financial Aid Conversation Guide

Financial-aid conversations should explain steps and ownership without promising eligibility, award amounts, or refund timing.

## Safe guidance

- Students may complete the FAFSA to explore federal-aid options.
- Packaging depends on enrollment status, program, prior aid history, and completed documents.
- Employer assistance and military benefits may coordinate with student accounts.
- Payment arrangements may be available for balances not covered by aid.

## Handoff triggers

Route to Financial Aid when a student asks about dependency status, verification documents, award amounts, aid eligibility, disbursement timing, or refund timing.

## Approved staff wording

"Financial Aid can walk the student through available options and required documents. Eligibility and amounts are confirmed only after the required review is complete."
