# Program Fit Quick Reference

Use this quick reference to compare common program families without overstating outcomes.

## Business and accounting

Business Administration is broad and management-oriented. Accounting is more structured around financial records, controls, reporting, and audit concepts.

## Technology

Software Engineering focuses on building software systems. Cybersecurity focuses on protecting systems and managing risk. Artificial Intelligence focuses on data, models, and responsible use of intelligent systems.

## Health and human services

Public Health looks at communities and populations. Human Services focuses on helping systems, case planning, referrals, and community resources. Clinical Mental Health Counseling is graduate-level, structured, and licensure-aligned.

## Education

Teaching English Language Learners is a focused graduate certificate for educators and support professionals working with multilingual learners.
