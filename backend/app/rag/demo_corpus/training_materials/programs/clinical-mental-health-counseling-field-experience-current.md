# Clinical Mental Health Counseling Field Experience - Current Admissions Guidance

Current admissions guidance says the Clinical Mental Health Counseling, MS is a 60-credit program with a structured course sequence. Students usually move through foundational coursework before field-experience courses.

## Timeline explanation

The program timeline is driven by the 60-credit curriculum, graduate pacing, prerequisite sequencing, and field-experience readiness. Staff should explain that the program is not just a list of courses that can be compressed at will.

## Field-experience talking point

Current admissions guidance describes a 100-hour practicum followed by a 600-hour internship. Because state requirements can vary, staff should avoid giving state-specific licensure advice and should route detailed licensure questions to the published disclosure and program team.
