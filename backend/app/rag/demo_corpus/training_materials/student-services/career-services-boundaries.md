# Career Services Boundaries

Career Services helps students prepare for employment conversations, but the university does not guarantee employment, promotion, salary, or licensing outcomes.

## Staff guidance

When students ask about career outcomes, ask which degree or program they mean before discussing possibilities. Describe fields, settings, or types of organizations rather than promising specific job titles.

## Salary questions

Do not quote salary figures. Direct students to public labor-market research tools and remind them that pay varies by location, employer, experience, and role.
