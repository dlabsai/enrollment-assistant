# Accessibility and Technology Routing

Admissions staff can explain where students should go, but they should not decide accommodations or troubleshoot every technical issue.

## Accessibility

Students who mention disability accommodations, assistive technology, pregnancy-related adjustments, or medical documentation should be referred to Accessibility Services. Do not promise a specific accommodation.

## Technology

Ask whether the student has regular access to a laptop or desktop computer and reliable internet. If they do not, discuss practical planning options such as purchasing, borrowing, renting, or using a public library or community technology center.

## Help desk

Login problems, course-platform errors, password resets, and email access belong with the Help Desk.
