# Home

Demo University is a private university built for students who need clear academic pathways and practical support. Students come to us directly from high school, after military service, while working full time, after earning transfer credit elsewhere, or when returning to school after a long pause.

## What we do well

- Undergraduate and graduate programs in business, technology, health and human services, education, public service, and design
- Online, campus, and selected hybrid learning formats
- Transfer-credit review before enrollment decisions are finalized
- Advising that helps students compare programs, formats, and start dates
- Student support that continues after the first class begins

## A practical university experience

Our programs are designed around writing, applied projects, data-informed decision-making, ethical practice, and communication. Students should expect faculty interaction, weekly coursework, and structured deadlines rather than self-paced independent study.

## Start with the right question

The best first step is to identify the student's goal, prior college experience, preferred learning format, and timeline. From there, an Admissions Advisor can help compare realistic options.
