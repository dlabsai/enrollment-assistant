# Learning Formats

Demo University offers online, campus, and selected hybrid formats. Format availability varies by program.

## Online

Online courses use weekly modules with deadlines, discussion, instructor feedback, and applied assignments. They are designed for students who need location flexibility but can commit regular time each week.

## Campus

Campus programs include scheduled class meetings and access to campus services. Some programs may include studios, labs, community projects, or other in-person learning activities.

## Hybrid

Hybrid programs combine online coursework with scheduled in-person sessions or fieldwork. Students should review travel and scheduling expectations before choosing a hybrid option.

## Format questions

If a student is deciding between formats, Admissions should ask about work schedule, commuting distance, technology access, and preferred interaction style.
