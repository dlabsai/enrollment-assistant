# Academic Calendar

Demo University uses different academic calendars by program and format. Students should confirm the start date connected to their program before planning work schedules, financial aid, or transcript deadlines.

## Upcoming starts

- Online undergraduate and graduate programs: January 12, 2026
- Campus undergraduate semester: January 20, 2026
- Selected hybrid health and counseling programs: January 26, 2026
- Late spring online term: March 9, 2026

## Planning reminders

Transcript review, financial-aid documents, and benefit approvals should be started before the intended term. A student may need a later start if required documents are still outstanding.
