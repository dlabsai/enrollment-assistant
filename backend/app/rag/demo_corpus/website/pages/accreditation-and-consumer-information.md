# Accreditation and Consumer Information

Demo University maintains institutional accreditation and publishes consumer information for students and the public. This demo page represents the kind of disclosure page a private university would maintain.

## Where to verify details

Students should review this page for current accreditation, state authorization, complaint-resolution, and consumer-information notices. Program-specific approvals or licensure disclosures may be listed separately when they apply.

## Admissions guidance

Admissions Advisors may point students to this page, but they should not improvise accreditation, approval, or licensure claims beyond the published information.
