# Admissions

Admissions at Demo University is consultative. The goal is to help prospective students understand whether a program, learning format, and start date fit their academic background and responsibilities outside school.

## How the process works

1. Share your program interest and preferred format.
2. Submit an application when you are ready to be reviewed.
3. Send official transcripts from each college or university previously attended.
4. Review transfer-credit, prerequisite, and admission-file questions with an Admissions Advisor.
5. Confirm financial planning and student-support next steps before registration.

## What Admissions can help with

- Comparing online, campus, and hybrid options
- Understanding transfer-credit review
- Identifying documents needed for an admission file
- Connecting military-affiliated students with benefits support
- Explaining who handles financial aid, billing, accessibility, and technology questions

## Application link

Students who are ready to apply should use https://apply.demo-university.example.edu/.

## Transcript reminder

Official transcripts should be sent to transcripts@demo-university.example.edu. Unofficial transcripts can help with early advising, but official transcripts are required before final transfer-credit decisions are made.
