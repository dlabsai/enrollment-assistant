# Transfer Students

Many Demo University students bring prior college credit. Transfer review helps determine how completed coursework may apply to a selected program.

## What to send

- Official transcripts from every college or university previously attended
- Unofficial transcripts or grade reports for early advising, when official records are not ready yet
- Military or workplace-learning records when relevant to the student's background
- Course descriptions or syllabi only when the records team asks for more detail

## What to expect

Transfer-credit decisions depend on the selected program, course level, grades, accreditation of the sending institution, and whether the prior coursework fits current requirements. A preliminary conversation can help estimate possibilities, but the official evaluation is what determines applied credit.

## Timing

Students should request transcripts early. Incomplete or inconsistent records can delay registration, financial-aid packaging, or degree planning.
