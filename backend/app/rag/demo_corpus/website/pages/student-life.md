# Student Life

Student life at Demo University includes both campus and online communities. Students can participate in academic workshops, student organizations, wellness programming, and career-development events.

## Student support

- Academic advising
- Tutoring and library research help
- Wellness and counseling referrals
- Accessibility services
- Technology support
- Career development

## Online connection

Online students can attend virtual events, join interest groups, and work with support teams through scheduled appointments and the student portal.
