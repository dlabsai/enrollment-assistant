# Academics

Demo University programs emphasize applied learning, clear communication, and responsible decision-making. Students should expect weekly engagement, written assignments, projects, discussions, and assessments tied to program outcomes.

## Academic levels

- Associate and bachelor's degrees
- Graduate certificates
- Master's degrees

## Learning expectations

Online courses are flexible but not self-paced. Students complete work within weekly modules and should plan consistent study time. Campus and hybrid courses add scheduled meetings, labs, studios, or fieldwork when required by the program.

## Academic support

Students can work with academic advising, tutoring, library services, accessibility services, and technology support. Admissions can explain the handoff, but enrolled students work with the student-support team assigned to their program.
