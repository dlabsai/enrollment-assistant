# Accessibility and Technology

Students need dependable technology access and a clear path for accommodation requests.

## Technology expectations

Most courses require regular access to a laptop or desktop computer, reliable internet, word-processing tools, and the university learning platform. A phone alone is usually not enough for sustained coursework.

## Accessibility services

Students who need academic accommodations should contact Accessibility Services. Admissions can explain how to request a connection, but accommodation decisions are handled by the accessibility team after appropriate documentation and review.

## Help desk

Students who cannot log in, access a course, or use university systems should contact the Help Desk rather than an Admissions Advisor.
