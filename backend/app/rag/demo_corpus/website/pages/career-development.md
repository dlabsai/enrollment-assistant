# Career Development

Career development at Demo University focuses on preparation, reflection, and practical communication. The university supports career planning but does not guarantee employment.

## Services students may use

- Resume and cover-letter feedback
- Interview preparation
- Career exploration resources
- Networking and employer-information events
- Guidance on using labor-market research tools

## Important boundary

Career staff can help students prepare and explore options. They do not promise job placement, salaries, promotions, or licensing outcomes.
