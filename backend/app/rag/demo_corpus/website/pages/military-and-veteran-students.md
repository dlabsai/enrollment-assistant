# Military and Veteran Students

Demo University supports military-affiliated students, veterans, spouses, and dependents as they compare programs and benefits processes.

## Helpful records

- Joint Services Transcript or other military education records when available
- Prior college transcripts
- Benefit documents or sponsor authorization when applicable
- Program interest and expected start date

## Planning support

Military-affiliated students should speak with Admissions early so transcript review, benefit routing, and schedule planning happen in the right order. Benefit eligibility and payment timing must be confirmed through the appropriate benefits office or agency.
