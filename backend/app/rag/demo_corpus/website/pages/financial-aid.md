# Financial Aid

Financial planning is part of enrollment planning. Demo University staff can explain process, timing, and offices involved, but eligibility and award decisions are made only after the required review is complete.

## Common funding topics

- FAFSA and federal-aid steps
- Employer tuition assistance
- Military and veteran education benefits
- Payment arrangements for balances not covered by aid
- Documentation requests and verification timelines

## What staff can explain

Admissions Advisors can help students understand the order of steps and connect them with Financial Aid or Student Accounts. Exact aid eligibility, award amounts, billing balances, and refund timing must be confirmed by the office that owns that review.

## Planning note

Students should not wait until the first week of class to ask funding questions. Financial documents, benefit approvals, and payment arrangements can take time.
