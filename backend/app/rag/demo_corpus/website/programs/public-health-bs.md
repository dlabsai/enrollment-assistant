# Public Health, BS

The Public Health, BS program introduces population health, prevention, program planning, and communication for community settings.

## Program overview

Students learn how social, environmental, and organizational factors influence health outcomes. Assignments include data interpretation and community-health planning.

## What you will study

- Foundations of public health
- Community health and prevention
- Health systems and policy basics
- Data and program evaluation
- Health communication

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online and on campus. Transfer coursework in biology, statistics, or social science may be reviewed for applicability.

## Related programs

Human Services, BS and Public Health, MPH are common comparison points.
