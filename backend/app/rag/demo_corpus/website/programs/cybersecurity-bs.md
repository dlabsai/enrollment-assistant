# Cybersecurity, BS

The Cybersecurity, BS program focuses on protecting systems, understanding risk, and communicating security decisions clearly.

## Program overview

Students learn computing foundations, network defense, secure design, incident response concepts, and governance basics. Coursework emphasizes responsible technology practice.

## What you will study

- Network and systems security
- Secure software concepts
- Risk management and governance
- Incident response planning
- Privacy and ethics

## Format and pacing

Online and campus options are available. Prior college credit in computing, networking, or math may affect course placement.

## Related programs

Software Engineering, BS; Artificial Intelligence, BS; and Cybersecurity, MS are common comparison points.
