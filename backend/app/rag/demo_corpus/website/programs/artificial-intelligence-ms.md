# Artificial Intelligence, MS

The Artificial Intelligence, MS program focuses on applied machine learning, intelligent systems, data governance, and model communication for professional environments.

## Program overview

Students deepen technical knowledge while practicing how to evaluate limitations, manage data responsibly, and explain model behavior to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

## What you will study

- Applied machine learning
- Data engineering for AI projects
- Natural language and decision systems
- Model evaluation and governance
- Responsible AI leadership

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online. Students without recent programming or statistics preparation may be advised to complete foundational coursework before advanced AI classes.

## Related programs

Software Engineering, MS and Data Analytics Graduate Certificate may be useful alternatives depending on the student's goals.
