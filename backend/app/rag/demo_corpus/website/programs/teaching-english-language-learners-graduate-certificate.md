# Teaching English Language Learners Graduate Certificate

The Teaching English Language Learners Graduate Certificate supports educators who work with multilingual learners in classrooms and community settings.

## Program overview

Students study language development, inclusive instruction, assessment, and family engagement. The certificate is not a blanket licensure guarantee; students should confirm requirements in their state or district.

## What you will study

- Language acquisition
- Literacy and content-area support
- Assessment and progress monitoring
- Family and community engagement
- Culturally responsive instruction

## Format and pacing

The certificate is offered online. It may be useful for teachers, instructional coaches, and education-support professionals.

## Related programs

Education electives and Human Services, BS may be relevant depending on the student's role.
