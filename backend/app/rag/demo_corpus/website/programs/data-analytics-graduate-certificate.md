# Data Analytics Graduate Certificate

The Data Analytics Graduate Certificate helps students build focused skills in data preparation, analysis, visualization, and decision support.

## Program overview

The certificate is designed for students who want graduate-level analytics study without committing immediately to a full master's degree.

## What you will study

- Data preparation and quality
- Applied statistics for decision-making
- Dashboard design and visualization
- Analytics storytelling
- Responsible use of data

## Format and pacing

The certificate is offered online. Some courses may apply to related graduate programs when catalog rules allow.

## Related programs

Business Administration, MBA; Artificial Intelligence, MS; and Software Engineering, MS are common next-step conversations.
