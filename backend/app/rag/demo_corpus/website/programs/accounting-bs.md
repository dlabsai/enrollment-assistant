# Accounting, BS

The Accounting, BS program builds practical knowledge in financial reporting, managerial accounting, taxation, auditing concepts, and business decision-making.

## Program overview

Students learn how accounting information supports planning, controls, compliance, and ethical decisions. Coursework combines core business study with accounting practice and communication.

## What you will study

- Financial and managerial accounting
- Taxation and audit concepts
- Accounting information systems
- Business law and ethics
- Data-informed reporting

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online and on campus. Full-time students usually take two courses in an online term or a standard campus load during a semester; transfer credit may change the remaining timeline.
