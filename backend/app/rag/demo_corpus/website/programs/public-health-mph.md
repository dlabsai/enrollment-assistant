# Public Health, MPH

The Public Health, MPH program focuses on population health, epidemiology, program planning, health policy, and evaluation.

## Program overview

Students learn to interpret data, design interventions, and communicate recommendations for complex community-health needs.

## What you will study

- Epidemiology and biostatistics
- Health policy and systems
- Program planning and evaluation
- Community assessment
- Ethical public-health practice

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online. Students should expect research reading, writing, data interpretation, and a culminating applied project.

## Related programs

Human Services, BS; Business Administration, MBA; and Data Analytics Graduate Certificate may be useful comparisons depending on goals.
