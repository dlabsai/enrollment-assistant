# Business Administration, MBA

The Business Administration, MBA program is designed for students who want to strengthen leadership, strategy, analytics, and cross-functional decision-making.

## Program overview

The MBA uses case analysis, group discussion, applied projects, and a capstone to connect finance, marketing, operations, leadership, and analytics.

## What you will study

- Strategic management
- Managerial finance and accounting
- Operations and project decision-making
- Marketing strategy and analytics
- Leadership, ethics, and organizational change

## Format and pacing

The MBA is offered online. Students typically take one course per accelerated graduate term unless advising approves a different load.

## Related programs

Data Analytics Graduate Certificate, Digital Marketing, MS, and Public Health, MPH may be relevant for students comparing leadership-focused graduate options.
