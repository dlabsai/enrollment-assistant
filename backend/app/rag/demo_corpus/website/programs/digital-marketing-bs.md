# Digital Marketing, BS

The Digital Marketing, BS program combines business fundamentals, audience research, content strategy, analytics, and ethical communication.

## Program overview

Students learn to plan campaigns, measure performance, and explain how marketing decisions connect to organizational goals.

## What you will study

- Audience research and segmentation
- Content strategy and brand voice
- Campaign analytics
- Consumer behavior
- Responsible digital communication

## Format and pacing

Online and campus options are available. Students interested in creative production may also compare Graphic Design, BFA.

## Related programs

Business Administration, BS; Graphic Design, BFA; and Digital Marketing, MS may be relevant.
