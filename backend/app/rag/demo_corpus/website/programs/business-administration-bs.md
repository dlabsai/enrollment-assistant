# Business Administration, BS

The Business Administration, BS program prepares students to understand organizations, markets, operations, and data-informed management decisions.

## Program overview

Students build a foundation in management, accounting, marketing, finance, operations, and business communication. Applied assignments ask students to analyze realistic organizational situations.

## What you will study

- Management and organizational behavior
- Marketing and customer strategy
- Financial and managerial decision-making
- Operations and process improvement
- Business ethics and communication

## Format and pacing

Online and campus options are available. Transfer students often bring business or general education credits, so transcript review is important before estimating time to completion.

## Related programs

Accounting, BS; Digital Marketing, BS; and Business Administration, MBA are common next comparisons.
