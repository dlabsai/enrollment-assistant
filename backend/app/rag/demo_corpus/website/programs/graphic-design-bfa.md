# Graphic Design, BFA

The Graphic Design, BFA program develops visual communication, design research, typography, critique, and portfolio development.

## Program overview

Students create design systems across digital and print contexts. Studio work emphasizes clear visual reasoning, iteration, and presentation.

## What you will study

- Typography and layout
- Brand identity systems
- Design research and critique
- Digital production workflows
- Portfolio development

## Format and pacing

The program is campus-based with selected hybrid coursework. Students should plan for studio time and portfolio checkpoints.

## Related programs

Digital Marketing, BS and Communication electives may be relevant for students interested in creative strategy.
