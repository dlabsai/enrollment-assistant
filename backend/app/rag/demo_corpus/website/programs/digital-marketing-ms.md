# Digital Marketing, MS

The Digital Marketing, MS program advances strategic marketing, analytics, campaign design, and leadership communication.

## Program overview

Students examine how organizations use audience insight, performance measurement, and integrated campaigns to make marketing decisions.

## What you will study

- Advanced marketing analytics
- Integrated campaign strategy
- Customer journey measurement
- Digital brand governance
- Marketing leadership communication

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online. Students should expect applied projects and writing-intensive analysis.

## Related programs

Business Administration, MBA and Data Analytics Graduate Certificate are common comparisons.
