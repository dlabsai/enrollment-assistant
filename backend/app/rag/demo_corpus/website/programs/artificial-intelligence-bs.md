# Artificial Intelligence, BS

The Artificial Intelligence, BS program introduces computing, data, and machine-learning foundations while keeping ethics and communication at the center of technical work.

## Program overview

Students study programming, data preparation, model evaluation, and responsible AI use. Projects ask students to explain how a model was built, what it can and cannot support, and how results should be communicated.

## What you will study

- Programming and problem solving
- Data structures and data preparation
- Machine-learning foundations
- Model evaluation and bias awareness
- Human-centered AI applications

## Format and pacing

Online and campus options are available. Students with prior computing coursework should request transfer review early because sequencing matters in programming and math-heavy courses.

## Related programs

Software Engineering, BS; Cybersecurity, BS; and Data Analytics Graduate Certificate are common comparison points.
