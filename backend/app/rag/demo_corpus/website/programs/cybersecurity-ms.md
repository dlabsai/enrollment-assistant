# Cybersecurity, MS

The Cybersecurity, MS program develops advanced capability in security architecture, governance, incident analysis, and organizational risk.

## Program overview

Students study how technical controls, policy, people, and business priorities intersect. Projects require students to evaluate risk and recommend practical improvements.

## What you will study

- Security architecture
- Risk and compliance strategy
- Cloud and application security
- Incident analysis
- Executive communication for security decisions

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online. Students should be prepared for technical reading, writing, and scenario-based analysis.

## Related programs

Software Engineering, MS and Artificial Intelligence, MS may be relevant for students comparing advanced technology programs.
