# Human Services, BS

The Human Services, BS program introduces students to helping systems, community resources, case planning, ethics, and advocacy.

## Program overview

Students study how individuals and families navigate social, educational, health, and community-support systems. Coursework emphasizes documentation, referral skills, and ethical boundaries.

## What you will study

- Human services systems
- Case planning and documentation
- Crisis and referral basics
- Ethics and professional boundaries
- Community-program evaluation

## Format and pacing

The program is offered online. Students interested in graduate counseling should compare this program with Clinical Mental Health Counseling, MS expectations.

## Related programs

Public Health, BS; Public Health, MPH; and Clinical Mental Health Counseling, MS may be relevant.
