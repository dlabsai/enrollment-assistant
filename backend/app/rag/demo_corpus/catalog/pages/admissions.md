# Admissions

Admission requirements vary by program level and academic pathway. Applicants may be asked for an application, official transcripts, identity verification, prior-degree confirmation, or additional program-specific documents.

## Official records

Official transcripts must come from the sending institution or approved transcript provider. Unofficial documents may support preliminary advising but do not replace official records.

## Program review

Some graduate, field-experience, or licensure-aligned programs require additional review before admission or registration. Students should not assume admission to one program guarantees admission to another.
