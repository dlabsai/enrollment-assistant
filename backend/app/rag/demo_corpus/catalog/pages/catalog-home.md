# Catalog Home

The Demo University Catalog is the official source for academic requirements, programs, courses, and academic policies. Website pages help students explore options, but the catalog controls curriculum and policy interpretation.

## How to use the catalog

- Review program pages for official credit totals and required courses.
- Review course descriptions for prerequisites and credits.
- Use academic policy pages for procedures, requirements, and appeal paths.
- Confirm catalog-year questions with the records or advising team.
