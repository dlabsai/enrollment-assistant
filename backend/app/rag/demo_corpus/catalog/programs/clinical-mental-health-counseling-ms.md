# Clinical Mental Health Counseling, MS

**School:** School of Health and Human Services

**Total credits:** 60

**Format:** Online with synchronous and field-experience requirements

The Clinical Mental Health Counseling, MS program follows a structured sequence. Students complete foundational counseling courses before practicum and internship courses.

- [CMHC 500 - Professional Counseling Orientation](#)
- [CMHC 520 - Counseling Theories](#)
- [CMHC 540 - Assessment and Treatment Planning](#)
- [CMHC 590 - Counseling Practicum](#)
- [CMHC 690 - Counseling Internship](#)

## Licensure disclosure

Educational requirements for counseling licensure vary by state. Students must review the state authorization and licensure disclosure page for their intended state.
